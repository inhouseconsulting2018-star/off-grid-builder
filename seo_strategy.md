# SEO Strategy

## In scope
- Public marketing pages
- Calculator landing pages
- Crawlability files (`robots.txt`, `sitemap.xml`, `llms.txt`)
- Social sharing metadata and structured data for public pages

## Out of scope
- Authenticated or user-specific app flows (`/projects/**`, `/results/**`, `/settings`, `/purchases`, `/proposal`, `/ai-assistant`)
- Admin pages (`/admin/**`)
- Payment return pages (`/payment-success`, `/payment-cancel`)

## Target audience
- Homeowners evaluating residential solar
- Off-grid property owners
- Contractors seeking a preliminary solar estimating workflow

## Primary keywords
- off-grid solar calculator
- solar sizing calculator
- solar battery bank calculator
- cabin solar calculator
- solar estimating tool for contractors

## Dismissed categories
- None yet
